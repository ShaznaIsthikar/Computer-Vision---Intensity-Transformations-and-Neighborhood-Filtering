Derivative of Gaussian symbolic verification

G(x,y) = exp((-x**2 - y**2)/(2*sigma**2))/(2*pi*sigma**2)

dG/dx = -x*exp((-x**2 - y**2)/(2*sigma**2))/(2*pi*sigma**4)

dG/dy = -y*exp((-x**2 - y**2)/(2*sigma**2))/(2*pi*sigma**4)

simplify(dG/dx - (-(x/sigma^2)G)) = 0
simplify(dG/dy - (-(y/sigma^2)G)) = 0
